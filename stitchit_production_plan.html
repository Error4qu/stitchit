AI integration opportunities
AI1 · customer-web
Garment description assistant
Customer uploads a photo → Claude Vision identifies garment type, color, fabric, visible wear → auto-fills description field in booking Step 3.
AI2 · customer-web
Service recommendation engine
After selecting services, call Claude to suggest 2-3 smart upsells: "customers who chose waist-in also add length shortening."
AI3 · customer-web
Natural language booking chatbot
Customer types "my jeans are too loose at the waist and too long" → Claude maps it to Waist In/Out + Length Shortening and pre-selects them in the wizard.
AI4 · admin-dashboard
Tailor smart assignment
"Auto-assign" button → Claude evaluates tailor workloads, past delivery times, customer location → recommends best available tailor with a reasoning string.
AI5 · tailor-portal
AI quality check on after-photos
Claude Vision compares before + after images → returns confidence score: "Waist appears fitted. Alteration looks successful." Admin sees this as a QC flag.
AI6 · backend email
Personalized email generation
Pass order details to Claude → get a warm, personalized email body: "Hi Priya, your blue kurta is ready. Your tailor Rahul has added a note…"
AI7 · customer-web
Price estimation from photo
"Get a Quote" flow — upload garment photo + describe changes → Claude estimates service category and price range. Converts browsers into booked users.
